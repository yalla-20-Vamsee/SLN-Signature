The Sign of LUXURY

Luxury within the city limits

SLN Signature is the upcoming project of SLN developers. The upscale project is located in Kompally which is just 10 KM from Paradise Circle. Out here life breathes luxury in every way. The Premium project embodies the spirit of a modern, hi-end lifestyle with impeccable standards and choices that bring to life exclusivity and ethereal charms. It is specially design for upper crust. So just say yes to SLN Signature and take your life to a new level of awesome experiences.

Specifications

  • Superstructure: R.C.C framed structure with Shear walls (RCC walls). Concealed columns and beams.
  • Designed as per relevant BIS codes for Earthquake Resistance-structurally efficient system implemented.
  • 10 feet wide Comidors and 10 feet ducts between 2 flats.
  • Doors WPC frames and skin moulded shutters for all doors.
  • Windows: Branded UPVC Windows with safe in-built Stainless Steel grill for movable window and sliding mosquito mesh.
  • Paint: Smoothly finished with putty finish and 2 coats of Asian Tractor emulsion for intermal walls & ducts. Textured finish with 2 coats of Asian Ultima emulsion for External walls.
